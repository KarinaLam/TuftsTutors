Math

At TuftsTutors, students gain a wider exposure to a variety math concepts
and the skills needed to solve complex problems.



For Students
  1. Struggling in math class? TuftsTutors is here to help you give you the boost you need
    to succeed! We have all gone through difficult journey in learning math and trying to
    understand complex math concepts. That's why we're here to support you and teach you
    how to conquer those challenges.


  2. We cover a wide range of topics, from the basics of geometry, trigonometry, algebra,
    to pre-calculus and college-level calculus, even extending to complex math theory! If you
    have a homework problem you are struggling on, you can be sure that we will have the
    knowledge and skills needed to assist you with your homework. Not only would we be
    helping you on your specific question, we would also work on covering and reviewing the
    key concepts needed to solve the problem. It will help you understand what you need to
    know in order to answer the question and how to approach it. This will rapidly improve
    your math problem-solving skills and your understanding of the math topics being taught
    to you.


  3. Want to learn other math concepts? We are also open to help you extend your math
    knowledge! We will work on increasing your math skills, helping you practice higher-level
    math techniques and application methods that will bring you ahead of your classmates in
    future math classes.


For Parents
  1. Is your child's math grades something your concerned about? Don't worry, TuftsTutors is
    here to help! Math is a subject that many students have difficulty with, your child isn't
    the only one! We will help our students with their homework and any questions that they
    bring in for us. Even if there are no questions, we will help students with their math studies
    and other topics that they'd like to practice.


  2. Wonder what a session with TuftsTutors is like? Generally, we would have the students
    bring in any homework problems they're struggling with at school. We would then cover
    the essential topics needed to understand the problem before attempting to solve the
    question. Once we see that the student has a solid understanding of the concepts, we
    would then begin to tackle the math problem. This gives the students a chance to think
    about what they know and do not know first. Afterwards, they will work on reviewing
    and mastering key concepts, which would eventually lead them to the solutions.


  3. Is TuftsTutors already sounding like the right place for your child to go? Click the button
    below to sign up! Still have more questions about our math program at TuftsTutors? You
    can still click the button below to send us your questions!
