Tufts Tutors: About Us

Setting every student up for success.


Tufts Tutors offers free tutoring services for students in grades K-12 in Mathematics and English. Our Tufts student volunteers will not only provide personalized academic support, but they will also ignite intellectual curiosity and serve as role models. We have tutors majoring in subjects ranging from anthropology to biomedical engineering, and students can be paired with a different tutor each time until they find the perfect match!
